Shameik Moore

Born in Atlanta, Georgia, Moore is a very talented and versatile American actor, rapper, singer, and dancer, with Jamaican descent. The 25 year old (born 4 May, 1995) started off with a few bit roles from shows like Tyler Perry's House of Payne, Read between the lines, and Joyful Noise. He landed his first lead role as Malcolm in Rick Famuyiwa's film, Dope (2015), which was produced by Forest Whitaker and Nina Yang Bongiovi, and narrated by Forest Whitaker. Moore has played many roles, in films as well as on television, throughout the years.

Film:

  • Dope (2015)
  • The Pretenders (2018)
  • Spider-Man: Into the Spider-verse (2018)
  • Let It Snow (2019)
  • Cut Throat City (2020)

Television:

  • The Get Down (2016-2017)
  • Wild 'N Out (2017-2018)
  • Drop the Mic (2019)
  • Wu-Tang an American Saga (2019)
